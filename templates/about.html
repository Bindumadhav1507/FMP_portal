About ADIA

The Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA) is a sovereign wealth fund established by the Emirate of Abu Dhabi, located in the United Arab Emirates. Founded with the primary objective of managing and investing funds on behalf of the Government of Abu Dhabi, ADIA plays a crucial role in diversifying and maximizing the emirate's financial resources.

Our Mission

As a sovereign wealth fund, ADIA engages in a wide range of investment activities globally. Its mandate includes the strategic allocation of assets across various sectors, geographies, and asset classes. The fund operates with a long-term perspective, aiming to generate sustainable returns to support the economic development and future needs of Abu Dhabi.
